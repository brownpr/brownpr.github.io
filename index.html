Optimised Design of Monopile Foundations

MSc Group project studying the non-linear behavious of Monopile Foundations. Tasked with coding the non-elastic behaviours of soils to predict failures of monopile foundations when under cyclic loads. Failure occus when the soils can no longer compact and breaks in a wedge shape, known as 'wedge-failure'.
